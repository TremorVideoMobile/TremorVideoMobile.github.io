- (void)adComplete:(BOOL)success responseCode:(NSInteger)responseCode;
Parameters
success
responseCode
Discussion

This method will be called when an the Tremor SDK has finished with the request to show an Ad.

responseCode -

kTremorResponseCodeSuccess:

ad showed successfully

kTremorResponseCodeNoAd:

No ad was available at the time

kTremorResponseCodeError:

An ad was returned but an error occured tyring to show it

success -

true:

ad was shown successfully.

false:

ad was not shown or an error occurred
